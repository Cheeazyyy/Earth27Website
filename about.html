Welcome To Earth 27

Earth 27 is a Discord community that was established in 2019 with the theme of Various Mythologies and Space Servers.
This is a means to find friends to play games or just joking together. The purpose of this Server
was formed to strengthen togetherness in cyberspace and eventually be able to meet in the real world
